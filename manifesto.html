Personal manifesto

Do good /
Do good by others, do good by you, do good by the planet, and do good work.

Never stop learning /
Keep a focus on learning and continuous improvement in everything you do. Travel, break frame, and stay inspired.

Work with people you like and admire /
Aim to work with people that care deeply about creating products that add meaning and value to peoples' lives.

Quality over quantity /
Focus on the few most important things, and do one thing at a time. Never rush quality.

Don't settle /
Maintain high standards and never lower them. Help others meet them.

Don't ask permission /
If it doesn't go well, ask forgiveness instead. At least you gave it a shot.

Design with purpose /
Always stay true to the problem you're solving, and who you're solving it for.

Keep it simple /
In life, and in design, a simple recipe usually works best.

Question assumptions /
True innovation and problem-solving call us to question everything, even our own biases.

Design from start to finish /
Great strategy means nothing without great execution. The best designers see a project all the way through to launch.

Respect the importance of space /
Keep space to breathe, space to focus, space to recharge, space to inspire.
